## Syntax

```java
ENERGY maxLevel [minLevel] 

maxLevel - Maximum level to consider valid for this trigger
minLevel - Minimum level to consider valid for this trigger
```

## Examples

```java
ENERGY 50
ENERGY 60 40
```
